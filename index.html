US Drought + Hydro Disruption — Third Compounding Pressure

The South was the hardest-hit. Drought + heat + wildfire risk = power grid stress = nuclear and gas power demand surge. Fire season threatens to reach grid infrastructure that is already stretched by Hormuz-driven energy costs. This directly benefits CEG, NEE, GEV, and natural gas infrastructure plays.
